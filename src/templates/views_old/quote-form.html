General Guidelines
  • We accept all types of quotes, whether it be online or in real life.
  • Please do not send in obscenities, profanity, or quotes that are disparaging to VT faculty.
  • Add context and comments with two slashes (// Comment here). Keep it short but complete.
  • Provide actions in between asterisks (*hits head on desk*).
  • Check your submission for spelling and grammar. Use standard English, proper punctuation, and common sense.
Please note:
  • Your quote can be viewed by anyone, including parents, friends, and faculty. Think before you submit.
  • We log your submissions and votes by IP address, but we can not identify your personal information.
  • Your quote may be edited in any number of ways, but we won't change anything important.
